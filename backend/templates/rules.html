Scoring Criteria

Each student starts with 10 points for each category

1. Punctuality

  • On-time (5-minute buffer)
  • Late (more than 5 minutes): -5

2. Participation

  • Asked meaningful questions: +1 (cap 1)
  • Distracted others: -1
  • Made a connection across ideas: +1
  • Challenged an assumption constructively: +1
  • Tried something new or took a learning risk: +1
  • Answered a question: +1

3. Teamwork

  • Contributed to team dynamic: +1
  • Makes sure all team members are included: +1
  • Allocates tasks to members: +1
  • Leader within team and/or able to follow the lead of others: +1
  • Helped a peer unprompted: +1

4. Adab

  • Includes others, spreads salaam, reaches out if anyone is alone: +1
  • Treats fellow classmates, instructor, volunteers with respect: +1
  • On phone or other electronics (except when required for the activity): -1
  • Student interrupts or speak over others or use disrespectful communication style: -1

5. Workshop-Specific Deliverable Evaluation:

  • Completion of the Activity: +1
  • Expanding on the Activity (investigation outside of what was covered in the workshop): +1
